Rupee Collector!!

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

Below are 4 different colored Rupees. Every time you click on a Rupee, your score will increase by a specific amount. Each Rupee is worth different amounts. You win the game by collecting different Rupees until your score matches the random number, but you lose if you go over!

The value of each Rupee will be hidden from you until you click on it!

Each time the game begins, the values of the Rupees will change! Good Luck!
